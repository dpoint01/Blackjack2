Blackjack2
==========

Simple Black Jack game running in terminal.

SAMPLE OUTPUT:

$ ./blackjack.rb
Welcome to Blackjack!

Player was dealt 10♦
Player was dealt A♣
Player score: 21

Hit or stand (H/S): s

Player score: 21


Dealer was dealt Q♠
Dealer was dealt 8♥
Dealer score: 18

Dealer stands.

You win!
